PROJECT 1

After weeks of a lot of learning, trial and error, and new discoveries, it’s time for a complex project, in which we applied all the knowledge we’ve acquired. It’s been a long journey but also a lot of fun.

Lazy Foodie is an app created for people who doesn’t know how to cook or the don’t want the same meal as always. With this app, you just type in the search bar the ingredients you already have in your kitchen and it will display to you several recipes that you can prepare with those elements.

Value Preposition

  • In just one click people can know what to cook with their resources.
  • A better management of the availbable resources in the kitchen
  • Make people aware of what they eat.

Technologies

  • Edamam API
  • Firebase
  • Materialize
  • Illustrator
  • Visual Live Share
  • Github
